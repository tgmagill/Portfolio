About Me

My name is Trent Magill, and I am 36 years old living in Chandler Arizona. I am a native to Arizona and cannot imagine myself living in many other places. I mean of course spending a few months somewhere to explore and learn about the culture and meet new people is always on the table but I like calling Arizona home. I think Arizona has a fantastic location and many different climates which to quickly escape to if you get tired of the bubble that surrounds you.Well, enough about Arizona this is supposed to be about me!

Currently, in my professional life, I get the opportunity to work from home as a consultant and specialize in the Real Estate industry. I love what I do because it is challenging and the Real Estate environment is continuously changing and evolving. It gives me the opportunity to bridge my existing knowledge of the industry with innovative ways to help organizations capitalize on their investments and infrastructure.

As a next step I have started to learn full stack developement. I have created this portfolio page to showcase what I am learning.  I will update my projects as my skillset evolves. So take a look at some of my work. If you have any questions or comments contact me using the form below.
